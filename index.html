Our Success Story

LC Plus is a 14 year old unique educational institute with a
potential based student centric approach and futuristic outlook.
The institute has pioneered the novice pedagogy which is a
perfect blend of academic and psychological development which
focuses on both the aptitude and attitude of the students. With
an enormous student base, the institute has served the city of
Bharuch with its heart and mind. Hundreds of happy and satisfied
parents and students have also encouraged us to explore even
further horizon of education. We are committed towards putting
Bharuch on the national education map by producing the army of
hardworking, honest and multitalented students to serve our
beloved nation. Jai Hind.

About the Founder

Mr. Pranav Vadia is a technocrat and Management graduate who is a
teacher by choice and not any compulsion. After completing his chemical
engineering at M.S. University of Vadodara and Management Degree
(Marketing and Finance) at FORE School of Management, New Delhi,
Mr.Vadia served a short stint of 2 years at the Head quarter of ICICI Bank,
Mumbai as Assistant Manager. Driven by the passion of teaching and
zeal of serving the student of his home town Bharuch, he quit a highly
paying job at Mumbai and founded Learning Center on 9th May,2009 at
Bharuch, Gujarat. Since 14 years now, he has been engaged in teaching
students, training teachers, exploring new educational technology with
only one aim in his heart and mind that the student of Bharuch must
excel and compete successfully in any competitive national level exam.
He is continuously striving to achieve is dream of putting Bharuch on the
educational map of India as national education hub.
